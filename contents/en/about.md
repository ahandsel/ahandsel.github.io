# About


Genji Fujimori is a [Kintone] developer advocate and a UX writer and localization specialist for a new product at [Cybozu].
A Californian living in Japan, Genji is now based in Tokyo.


## Work

* Developer advocate for Kintone and UX writer and localization specialist for a new product at Cybozu
* Focus areas: low-code web databases, developer experience and onboarding, technical writing, and Markdown-based documentation
* IT admin and consultant for small businesses
* Available to discuss low-code web databases and moving to Japan


## Career

* Studied Information Management and Technology at the University of California, Santa Cruz.
* Joined Cybozu as a new graduate in April 2019 and started learning Japanese.
* Created developer content for Kintone and worked with the Kintone USA office for five years.
* Migrated the Kintone developer community from Zendesk to Discourse.
* Improved team efficiency through automation and documentation.
* UX Writer (English) at Cybozu (October 2022-present)
  * Established English and Japanese content style guides.
  * Wrote UX copy.
* Global Developer Relations Team at Cybozu (April 2019-present)
  * Translated Japanese technical documentation, including API documentation, JavaScript tutorials, and developer relations promotional materials, into English.
  * Sponsored and supported hackathon events.
  * Managed the English style guide.
  * Hosted technical workshops.
